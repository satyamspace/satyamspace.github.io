About Me

Parinda Pannoon, is a project assistant at the Research Unit Cartography in the project related to AI and Cartography. In 2020, She graduated with a Bachelor’s in Geography and Geoinformatics in Thailand. She completed her Master of Science in Copernicus Master in Digital Earth program from University of Salzburg and Palacký University Olomouc in 2024. Her Specialization is geovisualization and geocommunication. She has a professional background as a GIS specialist, researcher and web developer. Currently, she involves in PeaceEye company as a freelancer with responsibilities related to web development, geovisualization and geospatial research. In her free time, She is keen to up-skill herself by joining several challenges or Hackathon competitions.
